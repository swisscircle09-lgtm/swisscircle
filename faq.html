Membership

Membership grants you access to our private community on Whop, which includes: live trade alerts with full entry/TP/SL details, daily market analysis, weekly educational content, on-chain briefings, and direct Q&A access with the Swiss Circle team.

VIP members additionally receive early-access calls before they're posted publicly, private 1-on-1 sessions with the lead analyst, and priority support.

We do not offer a free trial, but we stand behind the quality of our work. If you're unsatisfied within your first 7 days, reach out directly via the contact page and we'll review your case. Our track record is public — we encourage you to review it before joining.

We intentionally limit membership to maintain signal quality and community integrity. Swiss Circle is not a mass-market product. We keep our community tight so that every alert gets proper attention and support remains personal.

Quality over quantity — always.

Trading Signals

Every alert includes: pair and direction (Long/Short), entry zone, stop-loss level, take-profit targets, and an R-multiple target. We use R-multiples rather than raw percentages so sizing decisions remain independent of position size.

Setups also include a short thesis and the invalidation condition — so you always know exactly when the trade idea is no longer valid.

Primary focus is on BTC and ETH perpetual futures, with regular coverage of major altcoins (SOL, BNB, LINK, AVAX, etc.) when high-conviction setups form. We occasionally post macro spot plays during significant accumulation zones.

Quality dictates quantity. In an active week with clear setups, you may see 4–8 alerts. In consolidation or uncertain macro periods, we may post fewer or none. We never force setups to justify a subscription — no trade is better than a bad trade.

Access & Platform

Swiss Circle runs on Discord — all signals, analysis, education, and community discussion happen inside our private Discord server. Upon joining, you'll receive an invite link and be granted access to the member-only channels instantly.

Access is managed via Subscord, which automatically assigns your Discord role when your membership is active and removes it if you cancel — no manual steps needed.

VIP members receive alerts in a dedicated early-access channel before they're posted in the main signals feed.

Billing

Memberships are billed monthly through either Whop or Subscord — both platforms handle payments, subscription management, and your Discord role automatically. You can cancel at any time from your respective dashboard, and your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Both Whop and Subscord accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and select cryptocurrencies. Both platforms also handle your Discord role automatically — granting access on signup and revoking it if you cancel. Swiss Circle does not store any payment information directly.
