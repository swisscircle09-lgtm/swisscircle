Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — transparently.

Last updated: March 2026
Introduction

Who We Are

Swiss Circle (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a Switzerland-based cryptocurrency trading community, reachable at swisscircle09@gmail.com.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nDSG) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

This website does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or analytics scripts. No personal data is stored on our servers by this website itself.
01

Data We Collect & How

Contact Form
When you use the contact form on this website, your browser opens your email client pre-filled with your message. No data is transmitted to our servers. The email is sent directly from your own email account.

Membership via Whop
If you subscribe using a credit or debit card, your payment and account data is collected and processed by Whop. We receive only the information necessary to grant Discord access (email address, subscription status). We do not store payment card data.

Membership via Subscord
If you subscribe using cryptocurrency, your wallet address and email are collected by Subscord to process payment and assign your Discord role. Their own privacy policy applies.

Discord
Our community is hosted on Discord. When you join, Discord's own Privacy Policy governs the collection and use of your data within their platform.

02

Cookies & Tracking

This website does not set any cookies of its own. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking or advertising technology.

Fonts used on this website are loaded from locally hosted files — no requests are made to external font services that could track your visit.

Because we set no non-essential cookies, a cookie consent banner is not required for this website under Swiss nDSG or EU ePrivacy rules.
03

Purpose & Legal Basis

We process personal data only for the following purposes:

  • Membership management — to verify active subscriptions and grant Discord access (contract performance)
  • Responding to enquiries — to reply to messages sent via email (legitimate interest)
  • Legal compliance — to meet obligations under Swiss and applicable EU law

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

04

Data Retention

Emails and membership data are retained for as long as your subscription is active plus a reasonable period thereafter (typically 12 months) for account and legal purposes.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at swisscircle09@gmail.com.

05

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party platforms, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Whop — payment processing & membership management
  • Subscord — crypto payment & Discord role management
  • Discord — community platform
  • Google (Gmail) — email communication

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services directly.

06

Your Rights

Under the Swiss nDSG (and GDPR where applicable), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability (where technically feasible)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) at edoeb.admin.ch

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at swisscircle09@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For any questions, contact us at swisscircle09@gmail.com.
